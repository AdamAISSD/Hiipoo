## 4. Key Selling Points

### 4.1 Made for Sony CFexpress Type A Cameras

This is not an SD card. It is a **CFexpress Type A** card for cameras and camcorders that support the Type A slot, especially Sony digital cameras and cinema/video devices.

### 4.2 Up to 800MB/s High-Speed Performance

The card is positioned in the 800MB/s speed class, helping users handle large photo files, high-speed burst shooting, and 4K video workflows more smoothly than ordinary SD cards.

### 4.3 Suitable for 4K Video Recording

Recommended for recording formats such as **XAVC HS 4K, XAVC S 4K, and XAVC S HD**, depending on the camera model and recording settings. For XAVC S-I, 8K, All-Intra, or special high-bitrate modes, the buyer should confirm compatibility before purchase.

### 4.4 High-Speed Burst Shooting

Ideal for sports, wildlife, travel, wedding, street, and event photography where fast continuous shooting is important.

### 4.5 IP68 Waterproof Protection

The IP68 waterproof design is suitable for outdoor photography, travel, rainy weather, and challenging shooting environments. For professional listings, it is better to show test reports or official product documentation if available.

### 4.6 X-Ray Resistant for Travel

The X-ray resistant design makes it suitable for airport security checks and cross-border travel photography. Avoid claiming that the card is completely immune to all radiation.

### 4.7 Wide Temperature Resistance

Operating temperature: approximately **-15°C to 75°C**.  
Storage temperature: approximately **-40°C to 85°C**.  
Suitable for outdoor shooting from cold mountains to hot environments.

### 4.8 3-Year Replacement Service

The 3-year replacement service helps reduce customer purchase concerns. Sellers should clearly explain warranty conditions, non-human damage rules, purchase proof requirements, and whether data recovery is included.

## 5. Capacity and Version Recommendation

- **256GB / 512GB:** Good for photography, travel, daily 4K video, and general creator use.
- **512GB / 1TB:** Recommended for short videos, wedding shooting, commercial work, and users needing more storage.
- **80GB / 160GB / 320GB P-SLC versions:** Better for long-time high-bitrate recording and stronger sustained write performance.
- **Petpet 4.0 professional series:** Recommended when the buyer needs higher speed, professional video workflow, or PCIe 4.0 performance.

Seller reminder: **“Up to 800MB/s” means peak performance, not guaranteed full-time sustained write speed.**
